# Groww Weekly Pulse

## Top 3 Themes
- High Brokerage Charges: Users are frustrated by brokerage fees that exceed expected profit margins, eroding trust and driving churn.
- Execution Failures: Orders and stop‑loss triggers frequently fail, causing financial loss and undermining confidence in trade reliability.
- Feature Gaps: Requests for paper‑trading and kill‑switch controls highlight missing safety and learning tools.

## Voice of the User
- "charges are more than profit."
- "after getting SL I tried to take another trade to cover my losses but my trade did not executed"
- "please include a 'Paper Trading' option in this application."

## Recommended Actions
- Re‑price brokerage tiers to align with competitor benchmarks and introduce a transparent fee‑breakdown page.
- Audit order‑execution engine, focusing on stop‑loss handling under high‑volatility, and release a hotfix for missed executions.
- Prioritize delivery of a paper‑trading sandbox in the next sprint, with a kill‑switch toggle for simulated accounts.
